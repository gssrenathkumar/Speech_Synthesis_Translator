About Hackathon

Organized by the Department of IT&C, Rajasthan Hackathon 7.0 will be the seventh edition of this tradition that will offer a lucrative platform to the ingenious youth, startups & entrepreneurs across the nation to use their out-of-the-box thinking and bring innovation & change. Hackathon 7.0 will be a 24 hour non-stop coding marathon providing an excellent platform to brilliant coders, developers & designers to derive innovative solutions and take home prizes worth Rs. 25 Lacs along with an exclusive opportunity to work with the Government of Rajasthan. Coders, developers & designers will be given a chance to use their cutting-edge techniques and inventive methods on themes like Social Impact, Sustainability, Block Chain, Education & Learning etc.
